Collaborations

In Plain Sight

In Plain Sight presents anomalies in population distribution seen in nighttime satellite imagery of Earth and census grid counts produced by governments worldwide — revealing places with bright lights and no people and places with people and no lights. I worked as a research assistant for Laura Kurgan and the Center for Spatial Research on this project.
