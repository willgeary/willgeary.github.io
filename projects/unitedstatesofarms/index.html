The United States of Arms

Visualizing U.S. arms transfers from 1950 through 2017

About

The U.S. is the world's largest weapons dealer. In order to provide historical and geographic context to the U.S. arms trade, I visualized the flows of arms exports leaving the U.S. from 1950 through 2017. The underlying data is collected and published in the Stockholm International Peace Research Institute's Arms Transfers Database. Thank you to SIPRI for making this data publically available.

Units are expressed in trend indicator values (TIV). There is more information availale about TIV here. Each dot on the map = one TIV.

If you'd like to support my work and the creation of future maps that promote critical thinking, please consider becoming a monthly patron.

Tools used: Python, Processing (Java), Unfolding Maps library for Processing
