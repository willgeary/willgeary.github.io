U.S. Federal Government Spending

Visualizing U.S. Federal Government Spending from 1962 through 2023 (forecasted)

About

The U.S. allocates most of its discretionary budget towards military. In order to provide historical context to the U.S. federal budget, I visualized U.S. Federal Government Spending from 1962 through 2023 (forecasted). The underlying data is from Data from the Office of Management and Budget.

If you'd like to support my work and the creation of future maps and data visualization that promote critical thinking, please consider becoming a monthly patron.

Tools used: Python, Processing (Java)
